<p>If you're familiar with the OmniFaces library, you may know that it contains a number of useful utility methods. Some of these methods would also be<br /> useful in an environment where adding a dependency on a JSF utility library may not make sense, such as an EJB jar or a more general purpose library.<br /> That is why we are now announcing <a href="https://github.com/omnifaces/omniutils">OmniUtils, a general-purpose utility library</a> targeting Java 8.</p> <p>For current users of OmniFaces nothing will change, while there may be a small overlap in functionality between OmniFaces and OmniUtils, OmniFaces<br /> will remain a single-jar dependency.</p> <p>OmniUtils is currently in the very early stages of development and some major changes may still be made. Stay tuned to this blog for further<br /> information about OmniUtils.</p>
